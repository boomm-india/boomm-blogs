  When you live at home, items like Wi-Fi bills, groceries, electricity, and
  basic household items seem to magically take care of themselves. The moment

  A highly relatable 51% voted for "Everything costs money 💸". Watching your pocket money evaporate on basic survival requirements like metro card recharges and water bottles is incredibly stressful.   Learning to navigate the actual cost of living is a major stepping stone toward true independence. It teaches you budgeting skills that your future corporate self will deeply appreciate.
