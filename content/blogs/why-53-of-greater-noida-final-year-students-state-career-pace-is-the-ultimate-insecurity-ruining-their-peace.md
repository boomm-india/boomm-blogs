  who cleared which interview round. Instantly, a wave of anxiety hits you,
  making you feel like your career graph is completely flatlining compared to

  A dominant 53% voted for "Career pace 📉". The fear of moving too slowly in life can paralyze your day-to-day productivity. A student shared: *"I am genuinely happy for my friends getting placed, but seeing their success makes my own progress feel completely invisible."*


  Your early 20s are not an optimized speedrun. Everyone has different preparation phases, interview luck, and career trajectories. Moving steadily at your own pace is infinitely better than rushing into a job that doesn't fit you. Is career pace or income level stressing you more?
