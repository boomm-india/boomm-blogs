  Between assignments at Amity, trying to cross the Pari Chowk traffic jam, and
  maintaining a social life, college in Greater Noida can be chaotic. We used to

  We posted a poll on Boomm asking college students to share their "Biggest flex rn honestly". Given the choices between "Emotionally stable 🧠" and "Consistent habits 📌", a massive 61% of voters chose emotional stability.


  Let’s be real surviving college with your mental health intact is the real luxury. One user commented, *"Forget a 10LPA package, I just want a day where a single WhatsApp text doesn't ruin my entire mood."* Another added, *"My GPA might fluctuate, but keeping my peace is my main priority now."*


  If you are currently sitting in a Faridabad cafe or a Delhi metro coach replaying an awkward interaction, remember you are not alone. In a world full of academic stress and social media comparison, being emotionally stable is the rarest achievement.
