  For a long time, the ultimate college dream was securing a massive package
  with an impressive corporate title, regardless of the grueling working hours
  or toxic office environments that came attached to it. Times are changing.


  A telling 61% voted directly for "Low stress 🌿". Young professionals across Faridabad and Delhi are witnessing their older siblings burn out in corporate setups, making them value peace early on. An intern commented: *"What will I do with a high salary if I am too anxious to enjoy my weekend? Give me a decent pay with a healthy work culture."*


  Prioritizing your mental well-being over a numbers game is a sign of incredible maturity. A successful career is one that you can sustain without breaking your peace. Would you chase a high corporate package or a low stress environment?
